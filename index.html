ABOUT US

ECO TECH BASE (ETB) is a private entity (partnership) operating since 2013. Mainly working on renewable energy, Waste management as well as agriculture sector. we do research on agro-engineering and develop machineries suitable for the country. We are a leading character on hydro power development, biogas plant design and installation and conducting trainings (Locally and internationally) on energy technologies. We are working partnership with GOs, NGOs, Private sector and individuals. We have well qualified working staff consisting of an Engineer, Technical Officer, manufacturer, group of consultants, well-trained masons and field coordinators.
